After connecting to Notion, HappyCapy can help you read, edit, create Notion pages, automatically organize notes, generate documents, etc.

📝 Get Notion Token (4 steps to get it done)

  1. Create Notion Integration: Access https://www.notion.so/my-integrations
  1. Click the "+ New integration" button
  1. Fill in the Integration information:
  • Name:Just write whatever you want, such as "HappyCapy"
  • Associated workspace：Choose your workspace
  • Capabilities:The default is enough (Read content, Update content, Insert content)

4. Once created, copy the "Internal Integration Token" (looks like:ntn_xxxxxxxxxxxx）

🔑Give Integration permission to access the page

This is the most critical step! After getting the Token, you need to tell Notion to allow this Integration to access your page:

  1. Open the page you want HappyCapy to visit in Notion
  1. Click the "···" button in the upper right corner of the page
  1. Select "Connections" → Find the Integration you just created (such as "HappyCapy")
  1. Click to confirm authorization

📍 Get page ID

To operate on the Notion page, you also need to know the page's ID. Very simple:

  1. Open your Notion page
  1. View the URL in the browser address bar
  1. The last segment (32 characters) of the URL is the page ID

For example, this URL:

https://www.notion.so/My-Page-3065ab5584b5803e8572fcb253106e5a

The page ID is:3065ab5584b5803e8572fcb253106e5a

🚀 How to use in HappyCapy

After preparing the Token and page ID, use it in HappyCapy like this:

HappyCapy will automatically complete the operation for you!

❓ FAQ

Why does it prompt "page not found"?

Check whether page authorization is completed. Remember to add your Integration in the Connections of the page!

Do subpages also need authorization?

If the parent page has been authorized, the child page will automatically inherit the permissions. However, if the subpage is created separately, separate authorization is required.

Will the Token expire?

The Integration Token of Notion will not expire unless you manually delete or regenerate it.
