A few quick examples to get started

Get started quickly with HappyCapy through two simple cases.

📸 Case 1: Generate images and download them

The simplest example, get started in 10 seconds.

Step 1: State your needs in the input box

生成一张图片：可爱的卡皮巴拉在草地上晒太阳

Step 2: Wait for generation (about 10-15 seconds)

The image will be displayed in the dialog area and automatically saved in the outputs folder.

Step 3: Download to local

  • Method 1: Find the image in the file list on the right, right-click to download
  • Method 2: Just say "package this picture for me to download"

📂 Case 2: Upload files and process

Learn to upload files, HappyCapy can help you process various documents.

Step 1: Upload files (two ways)

  • Method 1: Drag and drop files directly into the dialog window
  • Method 2: Click the + sign on the left side of the input box to select the file

Step 2: Tell HappyCapy what to do

帮我整理这份 Excel：
1. 删除重复数据
2. 按日期排序
3. 生成一份干净的新表格

Step 3: Wait for processing to complete

HappyCapy will read the file, process the data, generate new files, and tell you what was changed. The processed files will be saved in the outputs folder.

💡 Quick Tips

  • State your needs clearly: The more specific the better
  • Say step by step: Adjust as you go
  • Give examples: Upload reference pictures, HappyCapy will learn
  • If you are not satisfied, say: Point out the problem directly
